## XI. MONTHLY TRACKING & REPORTING

### KPIs to Monitor

1. **Domain Authority** (monthly)
   - Check via Moz, Ahrefs, or SEMrush
   - Target: +1-2 points per month

2. **Total Backlinks** (monthly)
   - Google Search Console
   - Track: total, new, lost

3. **Referring Domains** (monthly)
   - Number of unique domains linking to site
   - Target: +5-10 per month

4. **Page Authority** (monthly)
   - Track for key pages (homepage, services, glossary)

5. **Organic Traffic** (weekly)
   - Google Analytics
   - Traffic from backlink referrals

6. **Keyword Rankings** (weekly)
   - Track top 20 keywords
   - Monitor improvement

## XII. BUDGET ALLOCATION

### Free Tactics (Time Investment Only)
- Directory submissions: 10-15 hours
- Guest posting: 5-10 hours per article
- Resource page outreach: 5 hours/month
- Social media profiles: 5 hours setup

### Paid Opportunities
- BBB Accreditation: $400-800/year (high DA link + trust badge)
- Trusted Choice membership: $500-1500/year (agent network + directory)
- Industry association memberships: $200-500/year each
- Local media advertising: $500-2000/month (includes online presence)
- Professional PR service: $1000-3000/month (optional)
- Link building agency: $1000-5000/month (optional, not recommended initially)

**Recommendation:** Start with free tactics for 3-6 months, then evaluate paid options.

---

## XIII. DO NOT DO (Black Hat Tactics to Avoid)

❌ **Never:**
- Buy backlinks from link farms
- Use PBN (Private Blog Networks)
- Participate in link schemes
- Use automated link building software
- Post in irrelevant forums just for links
- Create fake profiles for links
- Use AI-generated spam content
- Exchange links with unrelated websites

These will result in Google penalties and hurt your DA.

## CONCLUSION

Building Domain Authority is a marathon, not a sprint. Focus on:

1. **Quality over quantity** - One link from a DA 60 site beats 20 links from DA 10 sites
2. **Relevance** - Insurance, business, and local links are most valuable
3. **Consistency** - Small efforts every month compound over time
4. **Value first** - Create content others want to link to naturally
5. **Relationships** - Build genuine partnerships, not just link exchanges
