## TIPS FOR ALL OUTREACH:

1. **Personalize Every Email**
   - Use their actual name
   - Reference their specific content
   - Show you've done research

2. **Keep It Short**
   - Get to the point quickly
   - Busy people appreciate brevity
   - Use bullet points

3. **Provide Value First**
   - Lead with what THEY get
   - Not what you want
   - Make it mutually beneficial

4. **Follow Up Once**
   - Wait 7-10 days
   - Brief reminder
   - Don't be pushy

5. **Track Everything**
   - Who you contacted
   - When you sent it
   - Response status
   - Follow-up needed

6. **A/B Test Subject Lines**
   - Try different approaches
   - Track what works
   - Refine over time
