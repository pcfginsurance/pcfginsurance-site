## Essential Commercial Insurance for Buffalo Businesses

### Workers' Compensation

**Required by NY law.**

Buffalo's manufacturing and healthcare sectors have higher workers' comp rates due to industry risks.

---

### General Liability

Essential for all businesses to protect against customer injuries and property damage claims.

---

### Commercial Property

**Critical for Buffalo given:**
- Heavy snow loads
- Old building stock
- Freeze/thaw cycles causing damage

**Key tip:** Ensure your policy covers snow load damage.

---

### Business Interruption

Lake effect snow can cause extended closures. Business interruption covers lost income during weather shutdowns.

## Winter Weather Protection for Buffalo Businesses

### Essential Precautions

1. **Snow removal contracts** — Prevent roof collapses
2. **Heating system maintenance** — Prevent frozen pipes
3. **Backup generators** — For extended power outages
4. **Proper insurance** — Ensure winter damage is covered

### Insurance Considerations

- Confirm snow load coverage
- Check business interruption terms
- Verify flood coverage (snow melt can cause flooding)
